This is my project for 1.2.5

The game is Spongebob vs. Patrick Fireball.

How to play: move Spongebob and Patrick up and down using the “w and s” keys for Spongebob and the up and down arrow keys for Patrick. Each character has fire balls that they shoot at the opposing character. When one player has been shot 3 times, they are dead. A screen saying who won will appear right after. Everytime a character has been shot, their life counter will reduce by one.


Scratch Project: Animal Clicker Game




This is our scratch project. We chose to a clicker game and our theme is animals. Each animal gives you something different when you click on it. You start by clicking the chick (code is the first image on the left)and you will gain one point per click. Once you have enough points, you can click on the elephant (middle image code), which will give you one automatic point per second each time you click the sprite. Then, you will be able to afford the dog (right image), and the dog can give you two automatic points per second. Your points will continue to grow and you will eventually break the high score!
